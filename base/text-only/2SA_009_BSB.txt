You and your sons and servants are to work the ground for him and bring in the harvest, so that your master’s grandson may have food to eat. But Mephibosheth, your master’s grandson, is always to eat at my table.”
And Ziba said to the king, “Your servant will do all that my lord the king has commanded.”
